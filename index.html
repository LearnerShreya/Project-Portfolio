About Me

Hi, I'm Shreya Singh, a passionate and driven 2nd-year B.Tech student specializing in Data Science. I enjoy transforming raw data into actionable insights and building practical solutions.

My expertise includes Python, R, and libraries like Pandas, NumPy, and TensorFlow. I also have experience in data visualization tools such as Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Tableau, along with efficient data management using SQL.

Outside of academics, I enjoy working on real-world projects, exploring new technologies, and inspiring others in their data journey. Let’s connect and create impactful solutions together!
